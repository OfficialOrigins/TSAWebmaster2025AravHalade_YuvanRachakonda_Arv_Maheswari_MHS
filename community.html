Our Purpose

At Verdura, we are dedicated to minimizing our environmental impact and advancing sustainability in every area of our operations. The following actions will be taken by us in the following year to aid in achieving our goal.

Our Goals

At Verdura, we believe in sustainable practices that protect our environment. Through innovative farming techniques and responsible sourcing, we're working to create a greener future for all.

For every dish served at Verdura, we donate a portion of the revenue to support FWAF's initiatives.

LEARN MORE ABOUT FWAF
"Every meal served is a step towards a more sustainable and equitable community."

- Verdura Team

Nourishing Communities

We just don't focus on sustainability, we focus on our beloved community. Through our zero-waste initiative, we partner with Feeding Tampa Bay to ensure surplus food reaches those in need, making every meal count twice.

All surplus food from Verdura is carefully packaged and donated to support local families through Feeding Tampa Bay.
